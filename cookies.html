Cookie Policy

Last Updated: November 22, 2025

Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how CareerOption.Live ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website at https://careeroption.live. It explains what these technologies are and why we use them, as well as your rights to control our use of them.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and provide information to the website owners.

Cookies set by the website owner (in this case, CareerOption.Live) are called "first-party cookies". Cookies set by parties other than the website owner are called "third-party cookies". Third-party cookies enable third-party features or functionality to be provided on or through the website (e.g., advertising, interactive content, and analytics).

Why We Use Cookies

We use first-party and third-party cookies for several reasons. Some cookies are required for technical reasons in order for our website to operate, and we refer to these as "essential" or "strictly necessary" cookies. Other cookies enable us to track and target the interests of our users to enhance the experience on our website. Third parties serve cookies through our website for advertising, analytics, and other purposes.

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Essential Cookies

Purpose: These cookies are strictly necessary to provide you with services available through our website and to use some of its features.

Essential cookies include:

  • Session Management: Cookies that keep you logged in during your visit
  • Security: Cookies used to detect authentication abuse
  • Functionality: Cookies that remember your preferences like language settings

Note: Because these cookies are strictly necessary to deliver the website to you, you cannot refuse them without impacting how our website functions.

2. Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our site. They help us understand which pages are the most and least popular and see how visitors move around the site.

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Traffic Analysis: Cookies that help us analyze website traffic patterns
  • User Behavior: Cookies that track user engagement and session duration

All information collected by these cookies is aggregated and anonymous. If you do not allow these cookies, we will not know when you have visited our site and will not be able to monitor its performance.

3. Functionality Cookies

These cookies enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization. They may be set by us or by third-party providers whose services we have added to our pages.

  • Theme Preference: Remembering whether you prefer dark or light mode
  • User Preferences: Storing your career interests and saved roadmaps
  • Language Settings: Remembering your preferred language

4. Targeting and Advertising Cookies

These cookies may be set through our site by our advertising partners, including Google AdSense. They may be used by those companies to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant advertisements on other sites.

  • Google AdSense: Displays relevant advertisements based on your interests
  • DoubleClick DART Cookie: Used by Google to serve targeted ads
  • Remarketing: Shows you ads for our services on other websites

If you do not allow these cookies, you will experience less targeted advertising.

Browser Local Storage

Important: We use browser local storage only for essential functionality and user preferences.

In addition to cookies, we use browser local storage technology to enhance your experience. Local storage is similar to cookies but allows us to store larger amounts of data locally on your device.

What We Store in Local Storage:

  • Theme Preference: Your dark/light mode selection for consistent experience
  • Contact Form Data: Temporarily storing form data to prevent loss during submission
  • User Preferences: Career preferences and roadmap bookmarks (if applicable)

What We DO NOT Store:

  • Personal identifiable information (PII)
  • Payment information
  • Passwords or login credentials
  • Tracking data for cross-site purposes

You can clear local storage at any time through your browser settings. Please note that clearing local storage will reset your preferences.

Third-Party Cookies

In addition to our own cookies, we may also use various third-party cookies to report usage statistics of the website and deliver advertisements on and through the website.

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or by visiting www.aboutads.info.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to analyze the use of our website. Google Analytics gathers information about website use by means of cookies. The information gathered is used to create reports about the use of our website.

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps.

Cookie Duration

Cookies can remain on your device for different periods of time:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device until they expire or you delete them

Cookie Lifespan Table

Cookie Type Duration Purpose
Session Cookies Until browser closes Essential functionality
Theme Preference 1 year Remember dark/light mode
Analytics (Google) 2 years Track website usage

How to Control Cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can exercise your cookie preferences by clicking on the appropriate opt-out links provided in the cookie table above.

Browser Controls

You can set or amend your web browser controls to accept or refuse cookies. If you choose to reject cookies, you may still use our website though your access to some functionality and areas of our website may be restricted.

How to Control Cookies in Different Browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data
  • Opera: Settings → Privacy & security → Cookies

Mobile Browsers

For mobile devices, please refer to your device manufacturer's instructions for managing cookies in your mobile browser.

Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature that signals to websites you visit that you do not want to have your online activity tracked. Currently, our website does not respond to DNT signals or similar mechanisms. However, you can still control cookies through your browser settings as described above.

Cookie Consent

By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this Cookie Policy. If you do not agree to our use of cookies, you should change your browser settings accordingly or discontinue use of our website.

We may display a cookie consent banner on your first visit to our website, allowing you to accept or customize your cookie preferences.

Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time in order to reflect changes to the cookies we use or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Please revisit this Cookie Policy regularly to stay informed about our use of cookies and related technologies.

The date at the top of this Cookie Policy indicates when it was last updated.
