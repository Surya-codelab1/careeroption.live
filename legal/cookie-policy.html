Cookie Policy

Last Updated: March 27, 2026

Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how CareerOption.Live ("we", "us", or "our") uses browser storage technologies when you visit our website at https://careeroption.live.

We keep this simple: we do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics cookies. We only use your browser's local storage to save your theme preference (dark or light mode).

Do We Use Cookies?

No. CareerOption.Live does not set any cookies on your browser — not for tracking, not for analytics, and not for advertising.

We do not use Google Analytics, Google AdSense, or any other third-party service that would place cookies on your device.

What We Do Use — Browser Local Storage

We use browser local storage only to remember your dark/light mode preference.

Browser local storage is different from cookies. It stores data locally on your device only, and that data is never sent to our servers or shared with anyone.

What We Store

What Why Shared with anyone?
Theme preference (dark / light mode) So the site remembers your preferred display mode across visits No — stays only on your device
Contact form submission (name, email, message) When you fill our Contact Us form, we store your message so we can read and respond to it Stored securely and used only to respond to your query

What We Do NOT Store

  • Your browsing history or behaviour
  • Any data used for advertising or tracking
  • Payment or login information
  • Any information beyond what you voluntarily submit via the contact form

How to Clear Local Storage

You can clear local storage at any time through your browser settings. This will reset your theme preference and the site will revert to its default display mode.

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Clear browsing data → Cached images and files
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Clear Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Clear browsing data

Future Changes

If we ever add advertising, analytics, or other third-party services that set cookies, we will update this policy and add a cookie consent banner before those services go live.
